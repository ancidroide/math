# Integer Exponents

Summary of integer exponents rules and properties.

## Definition
For any real number $a$ and positive integer $n$:
$a^n = a \cdot a \cdot \dots \cdot a$ ($n$ times)

## Properties
- $a^n \cdot a^m = a^{n+m}$
- $\frac{a^n}{a^m} = a^{n-m}$
- $(a^n)^m = a^{nm}$
- $(ab)^n = a^n b^n$
- $(\frac{a}{b})^n = \frac{a^n}{b^n}$

## Zero and Negative Exponents
- $a^0 = 1$ (for $a \neq 0$)
- $a^{-n} = \frac{1}{a^n}$ (for $a \neq 0$)
